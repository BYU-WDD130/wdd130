We are River Lodge, one of the few backpacker-friendly lodges located between two of the largest and most diverse national parks in Honduras. Unlike most local accommodations, we are located directly on the Rio Cangrejal. The rustic character of the lodge gives it a cozy and homely atmosphere inspired by the surrounding jungle. Whether you want to relax and enjoy doing nothing, watch wildlife, or embark on a jungle adventure, Jungle River Lodge will provide you with an unforgettable experience.
