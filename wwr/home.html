White Water Rafting Corp.

During this tour, you will experience a sensation of tranquility and adventure full of adrenaline at the same time. First, you will hike until you reach a beautiful waterfall, and then paddle down the river in a raft.

  • Try some cliff jumping.
  • Hike the Bejuco trail to a refreshing waterfall.
  • Raft the class 3/4 rapids of the Cangrejal River.
