How we start

We are River Lodge, one of the few backpacker-friendly lodges located between two of the largest and most diverse national parks in Honduras. Unlike most other local accommodation, we are located directly on the Rio Cangrejal. The rustic character of the lodge gives it a cosy and homely atmosphere inspired by the surrounding jungle. Whether you want to relax and enjoy doing nothing, watch the wildlife on the rocks or embark on a jungle adventure, Jungle River Lodge will fulfil these desires and provide you with an unforgettable experience.
